Caprese Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

The Caprese Grilled Cheese Sandwich puts an Italian twist on the classic grilled cheese by adding slices of fresh mozzarella, tomato, and basil between two buttery, golden slices of bread. Itâ€™s melty, aromatic, and perfect for lunch or a light dinner. A drizzle of balsamic glaze can elevate it even more, making it both simple and gourmet.

Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices fresh mozzarella
  • 2 slices tomato
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Butter
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Butter the bread. Layer mozzarella, tomato, and basil on the unbuttered side.
  2. Grill on a skillet until golden brown and cheese melts.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze if desired.
