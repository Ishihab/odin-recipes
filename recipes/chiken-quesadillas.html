Chicken Quesadillas

Description:

Chicken Quesadillas are a deliciously cheesy and crispy Mexican-inspired meal that’s ready in just minutes. Shredded chicken and melted cheese are sandwiched between golden-brown tortillas, creating a gooey, savory treat. They’re easy to customize with extra fillings like peppers or onions, and taste even better when served with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on the side.

Time: 15 minuets

Ingredients:

  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • Salsa or sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet. Add cheese and chicken, then top with another tortilla
  3. Cook 2-3 minutes on each side until golden and cheese melts.
  4. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa or sour cream.
